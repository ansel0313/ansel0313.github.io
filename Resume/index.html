Laboratories and Project


2019.06-2019.11
Master Thesis: Depth Calculation and Color Reconstruction from Color-Coded Light Field with Neural Networks
Advised by: Prof. Dr.-Ing. Fernando Puente-León
The aim of this work is to estimate a depth map using methods of deep learning from spectrally coded light fields. With the help of the depth map, the spectral information of the central view can finally be reconstructed from the coded light field. Also possible is a joint reconstruction of the depth map and the spectrally resolved central view. In the work different methods are to be investigated and optimized, for example also considering different filter masks.


2018.04-2018.08
Laboratory: Advanced Control Techniques Laboratory
Institute of Control System(KIT)
The aim of this project is to explore the effects of various vehicle characteristics on driving dynamics. Therefore, a vehicle dynamics models was built in the MATLAB & Simulink simulation environment based on literature and experimental requirements. By testing the influence of parameters such as center of gravity position, steering stiffness, and tire characteristics on the dynamic performance in different test requirements, the vehicle parameters for different driving needs (sports, normal, safety) are finally designed.
